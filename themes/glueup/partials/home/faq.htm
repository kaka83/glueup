FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where in the world is Paygage available?

At the moment, we are available to vendors across the United States and rapidly expanding to EMEA and Africa. For information about your specific area, you can contact us at support@paygage.us or via live chat on this page.

Is Paygage PCI compliant?

Yes, security is our highest priority to make sure the money you collect is protected at each stage of the transaction until it arrives in your bank account. We invest heavily in securing our infrastructure and all data is transferred over encrypted SSL connections.

You can read more about PCI Compliance at PCI'S Security Standards Page or view PCI'S Frequently Asked Questions Page.

How soon will I get my money after the transaction is processed?

All transactions go directly into your bank account and they generally arrive in your bank account within 2 business days.

What kinds of payments can I accept with Paygage?

You can accept all major credit and debit cards such as Visa, Mastercard or AMex as well as PayPal and e-check/ACH.

Who are typical users of Payage?

Paygage is a payment gateway ideal for member-based organizations that have membership programs and run many events. Our clients range from multinational enterprises and event agencies, through chambers of commerce and associations, to start-ups and nonprofit organizations.

Can I see how Paygage is used?

Yes, we would love to show you how Paygage can benefit your organization. You can request a demo here or via live chat on this page and one of our specialists will be in touch with you shortly.

Do I need an EventBank account to use this product?

No, Paygage is available independent of EventBank. However, if you would like to automate your complete financial journey, we recommend using Paygage together with EventBank. Learn more about EventBank all-in-one software at eventbank.com.

Do you have special pricing for charities?

Paygage offers special discounted rates for confirmed 501(c)(3) charities. We also offer consistently low rates for all other nonprofits, associations, Chambers of Commerce and other member-based organizations.
