About Zoom

Zoom is the most popular video and audio conference cloud platform in the world with millions of users. It allows people to meet online “face-to-face” when meeting in-person isn’t possible via video conferencing meetings, audio conferencing, webinars, and more.

  • Run webinars and other online events in few clicks with outstanding sound and video quality even with slower internet connections.

  • Access detailed analytics from your webinars and online events, such as top attendees by meeting minutes and more.
