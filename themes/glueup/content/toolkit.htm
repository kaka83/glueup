What is changing ?

A new brand

EventBank is now Glue Up! EventBank has been transforming professional communities through digital solutions focusing on automation and engagement since 2013. The new name Glue Up represents the strength of our solutions, our growth, and our commitment to bring communities and engagement to the center of attention, making your success our mission.

Today, our platform empowers organizations in more than 50 countries worldwide, revolutionizing relationships, delivering long-term business value, and making modern engagement more purposeful.

With an intuitive SaaS platform, we are the leading provider of the cloud solutions that provide associations, event organizers, Chambers of Commerce, agencies, marketers, businesses, and NGOs with a full suite of tools designed to streamline operations, modernize processes, and eliminate engagement challenges.

Glue Up - Connecting communities

Our new logo is bolder, simpler, and better structured. It is composed of three dots, two of them interconnected. It represents the relationship between our software, the organizations we work with and their communities.

We want to convey the importance of the strength of that relationship between those organizations and their members as it is our main purpose.

Our new color scheme wants to bring strength and clarity to our message and increase accessibility making it easier to read for a wider audience.

Our new font is Ubuntu. Born as an open-source font developed for communities, we believe it represents our mission as well as bring a better character to everything we do.

The logo will be updated on the platform and our mobile apps. Our system emails will also include our new Glue Up logo. Download web-friendly versions of our logo in black, white, and color in multiple formats and learn more about how to use our logos in our new branding guidelines.

What changes for you as an organization

How will this affect my organization?

All event pages, campaigns, and other links will be automatically redirected to the new corresponding page in the new domain to ensure there is no disruption in the service. If users try to access the service using former EventBank links, they will be redirected to the new page on Glue Up instead.

Only if you are currently using our API, you will have to take action check the point below.

What happens with my published events?

All published events will be automatically redirected to new links. You don’t need to amend any links even if you have promoted your events on your official website, on a third party website or on your social media.

What happens if I have linked certain pages of EventBank on my website?

Your visitors will automatically be redirected to the corresponding page on Glue Up. If you prefer, you may update the links, but this is not a mandatory step.

What happens if I have integrated other apps such as Xero, Quickbooks, Zoom, or Google Analytics?

If you are using any of the integrations on EventBank, these will not require to be updated, you can continue utilizing them without amending the setup.

What if I am using an EventBank sender domain?

(i.e. info@companyname.eventbankmail.com)

This sender domain will be automatically updated to info@"companyname".glueupmail.com. This change will come to effect on September 7th, 2020.

Mobile App: Glue Up Manager

The EventBank Manager App is now Glue Up Manager. If you have downloaded the EventBank Manager App before, you don’t need to install or download anything again, simply update the EventBank Manager App from the App Store or Google Play. Then, log back in, choose your corresponding server and start managing your community from the palm of your hand.

What will happen to my contract with EventBank?

No changes are required, as our legal entity stays the same.

Update your API

If you have integrated certain parts of our platform with your website via EventBank API, make sure to update your endpoints from EventBank to Glue Up (the page will be redirecting to the corresponding server on .COM / .CN / .RU).

Make sure to update your end-points by November 24, 2020, as the temporary redirection of end-points will be discontinued after that date.

If you are currently using our API, you will have to take action. Make sure to update your end-points by November 24, 2020

What changes for your community

Login Information

On August 24th, 2020 all users will be logged out of the platform. To log back in you simply need to go to support@glueup.comGlue Up (https://glueup.com/login) and use your former login credentials. All your data stored on ‘My EventBank’ such as previous events, newsletters and memberships will be available in your personal engagement hub ‘My Glue', and the same will apply for all registered users.

How to communicate this change with my members, attendees and other stakeholders?

As with every change, it’s important to explain the impact of the transition to your community and answer any questions they might have. To make it easier for you, we have prepared a draft of an email campaign with the most relevant information that you can use to update your community about Glue Up and what it means to them. Feel free to use and edit this content to address them the announcement.

Mobile App: My Glue (end-user app):

The EventBank App for attendees and members is now My Glue. If you have downloaded the EventBank App before, you don’t need to install or download anything again, simply update the EventBank App from the App Store or Google Play. Then, log back in, choose your corresponding server and engage with your organization from anywhere. : iOS Store (https://apps.apple.com/us/app/eventbank/id958545893) / Google Play (https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.eventbank.android.attendee)

What should I tell my members/registered attendees/other stakeholders?

On August 24, 2020, your members, event attendees, and all other registered users will be logged out of EventBank and redirected to the new login page on Glue Up (https://glueup.com/login) that will display the information about the changes, and from where users can log in using their former login credentials they used on EventBank. The redirection will be automatic, so you don’t need to take any action.

I am a member of an organization, what happens to my profile?

If you are a member of an organization, you will have to log in from the Glue Up page (https://glueup.com/login). Your member details and access to the information from organizations you are a member of will be available there. If you have received emails to apply or renew a membership, you can still click on those links, and you will be redirected to the corresponding page on Glue Up to complete the remaining steps.

I have registered for an event, what happens to my future and past event registrations?

If you have registered for an event, you can still access your registrations via My Glue. You will have to log in from Glue Up (https://glueup.com/login), where your profile and all information related to your account such as previous events, memberships, subscriptions will be available.
