Over the years, we kept adding more features to EventBank and our platform became more than “just” event managment software. The company grew up and our software evolved to an all-in-one engagement platform offering so much more than what our old name EventBank would suggest.

What is changing?What does not change?What changes for you as an organization?What changes for your community?
