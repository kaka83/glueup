Professional communities globally are feeling the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic. Staying connected as well as keeping members informed are increasingly challenging as communities are not able to meet in person.

Now technology and digitalization are a must-do that no organization can afford to ignore. Professional communities need to change the narrative on their digital strategies to provide value for their members moving forward and enable their communities to thrive.
